About us

At Atiman, we are more than a service provider; we are a community. Together, we can build a brighter future for our children and youth. Whether you are seeking help or looking to support our mission, we welcome you with open arms.

About Atiman

At Atiman, we believe that every child and family deserves a safe and nurturing environment to thrive. Our mission is to provide comprehensive psychiatric support to children and youth facing mental health challenges, PTSD, depression, addiction, and other related issues.

We are dedicated to offering a sanctuary where young minds can heal, grow, and find hope. With a team of compassionate professionals, we provide tailored treatment plans that address the unique needs of each individual and family.

Who we Help

Children and Youth: Struggling with mental illness, trauma, or behavioral issues. Families: Looking for guidance and support to navigate challenging times. Caregivers and Educators: Seeking resources and training to better support the mental well-being of children and teens.

Our Services

Individual Therapy: One-on-one sessions to explore personal challenges and develop coping strategies. Family Therapy: Collaborative sessions to strengthen family bonds and improve communication. Group Therapy: Support groups where peers can share experiences and support each other.

FAQS

What age groups do you serve?

We provide support for children and youth ranging from early childhood through adolescence. Our tailored programs are designed to meet the specific needs of each age group, ensuring effective and compassionate care.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by contacting us through our website's "Message Us" section or by calling our office directly. Our team will assist you in setting up a convenient time for your first visit and answer any questions you may have.

What types of therapy do you offer?

We offer a variety of therapy options, including individual therapy and family therapy. Our services are designed to address a range of mental health issues. Our team will work with you to determine the best approach for your needs.
